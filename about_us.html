About us

Lensational Photography is a photography brand that primarily focuses on the coverage of celebrations of the milestone moments in your life. We are a bunch of young and ecstatic individuals with a deep love for photography. We feel thrilled working behind the lens as it gives us the feeling of possessing a superpower that freezes time; leading you to travel back in time to relive the most joyous instances of your life. We don’t believe in sticking to cliche methods and have a zeal to do the unusual.
